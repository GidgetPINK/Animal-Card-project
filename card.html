Lion

A lion's mane is a sign of overall health.
The mane also serves as a symbol of strength.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Leo
  • Average Mass: Male 420lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 10-14 Years
  • Habitat: Savanna and Grasslands

The lion is one of the most recognized animals in existence.
Males typically weigh more than 400lbs and their roar can be heard more than four miles away.
Lions are fierce prediators and equally beautiful creators.
